[Page 2]

Page 2 is designed for having a little more context in how to achieve a healthier lifestlye and what others do in order to achieve it. It provides a good base block for others to get an understanding of how it can be easily achieved. It provides the audiance with confirmation that they can achieve a healthy lifestlye without taking a life time to achieve. It shows the process of losing or gaining weight as well as how to build muscle if the reader is interested in it.

Images for the Page 2

[Page 3]

Page 3 is designed to give the reader tool/tools to help them achieve their goals. Provided on page 3 will be a calorie calculator which is very helpful to help gain or lose weight. It provides others with an understanding of how much they should be eating a day, and what is best for them. It will have a couple inputs like height, weight, etc. and will spit out their calorie goal for each day. Providing the reader with a base platform of how much they should eat in a day.
