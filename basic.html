So how do we keep a healthy life style when the best tasting food may not always be the healthiest? Its important for us to understand how to keep healthy while still being able to enjoy some of the food we love.

Gaining / losing weight

So how does weight loss and weight gaining work. How can we control what we look like? What weight gaining and weight loss is all decided on by how much calories we eat in a day. If we eat more calories than we need to maintain we store those calories as fat. If we eat less calories than the amount we need to maintain we will lose weight, we will end up burning either fat or muscle to help maintain.

So how can we use this to our advantage? We can control how much calories we eat in a day to decide on what to look like. If we wanted to lose a couple pounds in a month we could simply limit our daily consumtion by anywhere 300-500 calories below our maintainance calorie intake. This would lead us to start burning off weight and allowing us to start losing weight. The same is true if we want to gain weight we would simply need to increase our calories by 300-500 above our daily maintainance calorie intake.

Gaining muscle

Muscles allow us to do everything in life it helps us to move it helps us to react. We use muscles every day of our lives, from texting to eating. We use our muscles and our muscle memory to help us do everything.

So how can we build or gain more muscle? We build muscle by using our muscles and breaking them down. When we use muscle we are actually breaking down that muscle and ripping fibers, this may seem bad but its not. By ripping the muscle fibers we actually encourage the muscle to grow and become stronger. By consuming protein in our foods we are providing the muscle with fuel to grow more. The more we break the muscle down the more it will grow. Eating a good amount of protein in our diets and exercising our body allows us to grow our muscles more.

Basics of dieting

Many common beliefs is that once we start dieting we can never have the food we love anymore. However that is not always the case. Sure to get to peak fitness, and to look like the best body builders you would need to train a lot and eat the healthiest you can. However if you're simply wanting to lose weight or gain weight its not all that hard. You won't have to cut too much out of your diet to achieve that.

To lose or gain weight all you need to do is track how much calories your body need to maintain your current body weight, and either add or subtract 300-500 calories per day. Once you do so you should aim to eat close to that calorie intake. You can change how much you eat per day depending on your goals.
